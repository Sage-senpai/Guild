# Brand Name Strategy — AI Agent Marketplace

> Document date: 2026-02-28
> Context: Decentralized AI agent marketplace, Africa-first, global ambition
> Current working name: "Ajently" (MVP) | Candidate: "Guild"

---

## Naming Criteria

A strong name for this platform must:
1. **Be globally pronounceable** — across English, Swahili, French, Yoruba, Arabic markets
2. **Imply ownership or agency** — creators own agents; names should feel empowering
3. **Be Web3-ready** — able to carry a token ticker; no confusion with existing protocols
4. **Have domain availability** — `.io`, `.xyz`, or `.co` available or acquirable
5. **Evoke the product** — marketplace, agents, intelligence, or decentralization
6. **Age well** — not trend-dependent; no forced AI/crypto buzzwords

### GROUP A: 5 Premium Brandable Names

---

#### 1. GUILD ⭐ (Top Candidate)

**Meaning**: A historical association of craftspeople who set standards, trained members, shared resources, and collectively prospered. Guilds were the original creator economies.

**Product fit**: Perfect. Agents are the "craftspeople." The marketplace is the guild. Creators join the guild to publish; users access the guild's work. The historical guild model is explicitly decentralized — no single authority, peer governance, shared standards.

**Africa resonance**: Guild structures (cooperative/chama in Kenya, susu in West Africa, stokvel in South Africa) are deeply embedded in African economic culture. "Guild" will feel familiar and aspirational.

**Domain likelihood**:
- `guild.io` — likely taken; may be acquirable ($5K-$50K range)
- `guild.xyz` — likely available or cheap
- `guildprotocol.xyz` — likely available
- `theguild.io` — likely available

**Token ticker**: `GUILD` ✓ — clean, no conflicts
**Branding**: The G could form a shield/compass mark — premium, timeless
**Tagline**: "Your craft. Your agents. Your guild."

### GROUP C: 3 Africa-Rooted, Global-Scalable Names

---

#### 9. UJUZI ⭐

**Meaning**: "Knowledge" or "skill" in Swahili. Pronounced: oo-JOO-zee.

**Product fit**: Excellent. An agent marketplace is literally a marketplace for ujuzi — packaged knowledge and skill. The name is honest and direct.

**Africa resonance**: Swahili is spoken by 200M+ people across East Africa (Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, Rwanda, DRC, Mozambique). Instantly meaningful to the target user base. Globally unique — no existing Web3 project uses this name.

**Global accessibility**: Short, phonetically consistent, memorable. Non-Swahili speakers find it exotic and distinctive — an asset for global branding.

**Domain likelihood**:
- `ujuzi.io` — likely available (very underutilized in tech)
- `ujuzi.co` — likely available
- `ujuzi.xyz` — likely available

**Token ticker**: `UJUZI` ✓ — unique, no conflicts
**Branding**: Could incorporate Maasai-inspired geometric patterns or Adinkra symbols
**Tagline**: "Knowledge. Deployed."

#### 10. SANKOFA

**Meaning**: Akan concept from Ghana: "Se wo were fi na wosankofa a yenkyi" — "It is not wrong to go back for what you forgot." Represented by a bird looking backward while moving forward. Symbolizes learning from the past to build the future.

**Product fit**: Moderate to strong. The AI agent carries stored knowledge (looking back) to help users move forward. Deep philosophical alignment.

**Africa resonance**: Sankofa is pan-African — adopted as a symbol across the diaspora and African continent. Has global recognition in African cultural spaces.

**Caution**: Strong cultural symbol — must be used respectfully. Do not use as purely aesthetic; integrate the philosophy into product values.

**Domain likelihood**:
- `sankofa.io` — likely available
- `sankofai.io` — likely available

**Token ticker**: `SANKO` ✓ (distinctive)
**Tagline**: "Learn from the past. Build the future."

## Recommendation

### Primary Recommendation: **GUILD**

Guild is timeless, premium, globally accessible, deeply meaningful (cooperative economy model), and carries strong resonance with Africa's cooperative economic traditions. It is the strongest brand for global expansion while retaining cultural depth.

**Next steps**:
1. Check `guild.io` WHOIS; reach out to current owner if taken
2. Register `guildprotocol.xyz` and `guild.xyz` immediately
3. Register `@guildprotocol` on Twitter/X, GitHub, Discord
4. File trademark application in US + EU + Kenya/Nigeria + UK

### Secondary Recommendation: **UJUZI**

If GUILD is unavailable or prohibitively expensive, UJUZI is the strongest alternative. It is unique in the global tech ecosystem, meaningful to the target user base, and will become increasingly recognizable as the platform grows. The Africa-first origin story becomes a central part of the brand — not an afterthought.

**Tagline pair**:
- GUILD: *"Your craft. Your agents. Your guild."*
- UJUZI: *"Knowledge, deployed."*
